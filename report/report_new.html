COVID-19 in New Zealand

The course of the pandemic, vaccinations, and correlation between indicators

New Zealand adopted an elimination strategy for COVID-19 early in the pandemic, resulting in a low number of cases and deaths compared to other countries. Effective border measures, swift lockdowns, and a high vaccination rate played a crucial role in this strategy.
